# Living Wiki

A self-improving knowledge base built on Obsidian + Claude Code. You brain-dump everything; Claude organizes it automatically. Open source framework anyone can clone.

## What It Is

Three operating loops:
1. **Ingestion** -- stream-of-consciousness or external content → organized, linked wiki notes
2. **Query + Filing** -- answers compound; previous queries become source material
3. **Maintenance** -- health checks for broken links, orphaned notes, stale content
